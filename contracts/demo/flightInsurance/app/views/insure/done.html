Quick FAQ

What if I type the wrong flight number?

After your policy request is processed, your premium will be returned minus a small processing fee.

What if I book a flight on the wrong date?

Unfortunately, we do not currently support cancellations. Please carefully check that the date is correct when purchasing your insurance.

Can I use this to buy insurance for my flight on Thursday?

Big Red Flight Insurance is currently a prototype and makes no guarantees about continued operation. Please use at your own risk.

What if my flight is canceled completely?

A canceled flight will pay out as if it was delayed.

I can't see my policy in the list!

It may take several seconds for the list to update to reflect your policy. Try refreshing your policy list page, and if it still does not show up check a blockchain explorer to see if your policy ID was included in the chain.
